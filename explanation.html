What we're all about!




Hello creators!

Welcome to the creators corner, on this site you will find all of the tools you need to be successful in your social media management endeavor. whether you are a social media manager, inspiring influencer, or a budding business you will find everything you need here at your disposal. we have graphs, more graphs, and even MORE graphs! there are profiles to create, forums to join, forms for use, and e-commerce help for those that need it.

Profiles - with this dynamic profile people can see what you are all about, all of your socials will be on display as well as your contact information if you wish. Fill your bio with relevant information that will capture the readers attention, and make them want to hire you, or collaborate with you directly from your page.

Charts - we have all kind of charts, and we mean all kinds! bar charts, wave charts, line charts, pie charts, donut charts, gague charts, and even spark line charts. That's right we named every chart type we have! Any data you need will be at your disposal so yuo an make an accurate decision about your performance on each platform. Later on we will add the ability to print reports from this information.

E-Commerce - Later on we will add the ability to connect your online store and Instagram store so that users can order directly off of your page. We will accept every kind of payment and if plans are correct we will also accept Bitcoin.

Social Pages and Forums - Immerse yourself in the forums and absorb all of the knowledge of your peers. Find collabortions in our special collab tab, or share your knowledge in any of the other popular hashtags. The forum is where knowledge gathers so it is an invaluable resource for those looking for informaion.

The social pages allows you to plan content and see how your posts are doing. you can connect your posting schedule to your calander to get notifications that keep you on track for your postings. you can schedule posts to post at a specific date or time period so you do not have to be overly activ worrying about posting at the same time every other day. In the future we will have a algorithm that takes your best performing posts, analyze it, then recreate a similar post so that all you have to do is make slight tweaks and pop it into the schedule.

We look forward to see everyone enjoy the site! We are always pen for suggestions so feel free to fill out the suggestions form and we will read over all of them to see what we can improve on or add to the site that could make everyones life easier.
Thank you!
Justus Harris, Founder
